How it works

Capture

Records system audio from Zoom, Teams, Meet, and any other app, with your mic mixed in. Lives in the menu bar.

Transcribe

Instant transcription using Apple Speech Recognition during recording. WhisperKit available as fallback.

Summarize

Local LLM generates structured summaries with key decisions, action items, and discussion highlights.

Auto-record

Optionally starts recording when Zoom or Teams launches, or when a calendar event begins. Stops on silence.

Calendar integration

Connects to Apple Calendar or Google Calendar to identify participants and attribute action items.

Custom models

Use built-in MLX models or add any HuggingFace model. Ollama also supported for local or remote inference.

Requirements

  • macOS 14.2+ (Sonoma) — required for ScreenCaptureKit audio capture
  • Apple Silicon — M1 minimum, M2 Pro+ recommended
  • 16 GB RAM minimum — 32 GB recommended for LLM summarization
